Where We're Going

Grocery

Woodman's Market

W124N8145 Hwy 145, Menomonee Falls, WI 53051

Daily 6:00 AM - 9:45 PM

Massive Wisconsin grocery chain with incredible selection. Stock up on local cheese, beer, and Wisconsin specialties. Don't miss their bakery and deli sections!

Specialty Shop

Usinger's Famous Sausage

1030 N Old World 3rd St, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Mon-Sat 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Milwaukee institution since 1880. Authentic German sausages, bratwurst, and summer sausage. Try their traditional weisswurst and take home some landjaeger!

Restaurant

Zaffiro's Pizza

1724 N Farwell Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Mon-Sun 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

$$ - Pizzas $12-20

Milwaukee-style thin crust pizza legend since 1954. Crispy, square-cut pies loaded with cheese. Order the classic sausage and mushroom!

Specialty Shop

Wisconsin Cheese Mart

215 W Highland Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Mon-Sat 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Wisconsin's finest artisan cheeses under one roof. Fresh curds, aged cheddars, and unique local varieties. Perfect for gifts and picnic supplies!

Attraction

Lambeau Field

1265 Lombardi Ave, Green Bay, WI 54304

Tours: Mon-Sat 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sun 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Adults $18, Seniors $15, Youth $12

Day trip to see the legendary Packers stadium! Hall of Fame and stadium tour. Plan 2-3 hours. About 2 hours north of Milwaukee. Go Pack Go! 🏈

Bakery

Simma's Bakery

817 N 68th St, Wauwatosa, WI 53213

Tue-Fri 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Family bakery specializing in European pastries. Don't miss the cream puffs, strudels, and authentic German cakes. Call ahead for special orders!

Bakery

Grebe's Bakery

5132 W Lincoln Ave, West Allis, WI 53219

Tue-Sun 5:00 AM - 3:00 PM (Closed Monday)

Old-fashioned donuts made fresh daily since 1937. Get there early! Famous for their glazed, cake donuts, and seasonal specialties. Pure Milwaukee tradition.

Coffee Shop

Colectivo Coffee

6745 W Wells St, Wauwatosa, WI 53213

Mon-Fri 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Sat-Sun 6:30 AM - 6:00 PM

Milwaukee's beloved local roaster. Trendy atmosphere, excellent espresso, and house-baked pastries. Great morning spot or afternoon coffee break!

Restaurant

Balistreri's Pizza

812 N 68th St, Wauwatosa, WI 53213

Wed-Mon 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM (Closed Tuesday)

$$ - Pizzas $14-22

Classic Milwaukee thin crust pizza and Italian-American favorites. Family-owned since 1962. Try the Italian sausage pizza or baked mostaccioli!

Pub

O'Brien's Pub

4928 W Vliet St, Milwaukee, WI 53208

Tue-Sun 3:00 PM - 2:00 AM (Closed Monday)

$ - Budget-friendly

Neighborhood Irish-American pub with Thursday tradition! Great beer selection, pub food, shuffleboard, and darts. Local favorite with a welcoming vibe.

Brewery

Lakefront Brewery

1872 N Commerce St, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Famous brewery tour with a beer hall vibe. Known for their Riverwest Stein Beer and Friday Fish Fry tradition.

Brewery

Milwaukee Brewing Company

613 S 2nd St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Craft brewery in Walker's Point. Must-try: O-Gii Belgian-Style Wit and their seasonal German-inspired lagers.

Restaurant

Mader's Restaurant

1041 N Old World 3rd St, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Historic German restaurant since 1902. Schnitzel, sauerbraten, and an incredible beer stein collection.

Restaurant

The Old German Beer Hall

1009 N Old World 3rd St, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Authentic beer hall atmosphere with communal tables, live polka, and massive pretzels.

Attraction

German Fest Grounds

Henry Maier Festival Park, Milwaukee, WI 53207

The main event! Four days of German music, food, beer, and culture on the lakefront.

Food & Drink Highlights

Bratwurst

Classic German sausage grilled to perfection. Served on a fresh roll with sauerkraut and spicy mustard. A German Fest staple!

Giant Pretzel

Authentic Bavarian soft pretzel the size of your head. Warm, salty, perfect with beer cheese dip or sweet mustard.

Wisconsin Cheese Curds

Fresh, squeaky cheese curds — a Wisconsin delicacy. Even better when beer-battered and deep-fried to golden perfection.

Schnitzel

Breaded and pan-fried pork or veal cutlet. Crispy on the outside, tender inside. Served with lemon and lingonberry sauce.

German Potato Salad

Warm potato salad with bacon, vinegar, and mustard. Tangy, savory, and the perfect side dish for any German meal.

Strudel

Traditional apple strudel with flaky pastry, cinnamon apples, and raisins. Best served warm with vanilla ice cream.

German Lager

Crisp, clean, refreshing. Milwaukee's German brewing heritage shines through in every pour. Prost!

Hefeweizen

Cloudy wheat beer with banana and clove notes. Light, refreshing, perfect for summer festival drinking.
